From Our Founder

A Note from Aahana

I started Bridge the Gap Robotics Foundation with a simple but powerful belief: that the zip code a child grows up in should never determine how far they can go.

Our mission is to democratize access to STEM education by building kits, running workshops, and bringing hands-on learning experiences to underserved communities. We believe that when a child holds a robotics kit, assembles a mechanical arm, or writes their first line of code, something shifts. They stop seeing science as something that happens elsewhere and start seeing it as something they can do.

To every leader reading this: you are not just running a chapter or filling a role on a board. You are a steward of this mission. You are the reason a child in your community will one day pick up a kit, feel the spark of curiosity, and believe they belong in STEM.

Aahana Basappa Founder & Executive Director, Bridge the Gap Robotics Foundation
